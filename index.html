Fitness

This page explains the basics of fitness and healthy living.

Why Fitness Is Important

While fitness is often associated with bodybuilding, there are numerous health benefits unrealted to building muscle. For example, fitness helps improve physical health, mental well-being, and overall quality of life.

Regular exercise strengthens the heart, increases energy, and reduces stress.

Go get active!

Most think it's hard to engage in fitness, but staying active can include gym workouts, outdoor exercise, or simple daily movement.

Benefits of Exercise

  • Improves heart health
  • Builds muscle strength & mass
  • Boosts mental health
